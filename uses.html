Uses!

In today's society, robots help humans daily. Some of these are medical, professional and cleaning. Typically, it is used to make the lives of humans a little easier. When learning about the uses of robots, there are many kinds of robots that do the same thing, whether it be entertainment, service or medical. Let's look into it some more shall we?

List of Uses & Their Functions

*PLEASE NOTE THIS ISN'T EVER SINGLE USE FOR A ROBOT. THERE ARE A VAST MAJORITY OF USES AND I CHOSE THE ONES THAT ARE MOST COMMON AND/OR USED IN DAY TO DAY LIFE.*

Cleaning - Some robots like the Roomba® Robot Vacuums, are used to help speed up the process of cleaning, making human lives a little better knowing that something else is helpping them out. Some examples are: Washing Machines, Dishwashers & Roomba® Robot Vacuums.


Health - These robots are trained and programmed to do hard surgicial tasks that even trained professionals have a hard time of doing. These robots are used in non-evasive surgires and help to improve accuracy and the sucess rate of the surgey itself. Some of these surgires are: Cardiovascular surgery, Head and neck surgery, Gynaecologic surgery, Partial knee replacement surgery and more.


Communications & Money - Robots are actually used for communication and money as well! An ATM is considered a robot since it is an automated machine that does not require the use of a human. Siri, Apple's virtual assistant is considered a robot as well since it doesn't require a human to operate.


Transport - Robots are used in transport in order to bring packages from an end of a warehouse to another or even to serve you food from a local resutrant. Amazon has a robot that helps to move packages to the right area simply called "drives".


Entertainment - Robots are used for the entertainmentof children young and old. This may be a robotic dog that someone got as a gift or even the LOVOT bot mentioned in the information tab. These robots do not have any other purpose than to entertain and amuse a person, which is why they are fairly widespread to the public. All the rest of the uses minus health are seen in day-to-day life, but not as extreme as the entertainment side of a robot.
